Put statements in positive form

Avoid tame, colorless, hesitating, irresolute language, all of which makes you boring.
Bianca does not remain long in memory as an important character in Shakespeare's works.
Bianca is an insignificant character in Shakespeare's works.
If every statement admits a doubt, writing/speaking will lack authority. Save would, should, could, may, might and can for situations envolving real uncertainty.

Use definite, specific, concrete language

Prefer specific to general, definite to vauge, concrete rather than abstract.
